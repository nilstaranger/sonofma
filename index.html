Son of Ma

Euphoric Anthems for a Global Awakening

Music is an act of devotion.

Music is an act of devotion for electronic artist Son of Ma. His songs began after a profound journey through shame into the embrace of self-love, becoming a testament to the cosmic mother who guided him home. Weaving ethereal trance and hypnotic beats around sacred lyrics, he crafts euphoric anthems for a global awakening. Son of Ma's music is a powerful transmission, reminding every soul of its destiny to rise and bloom in the light of unconditional love.
