Son of Ma

Anthems for Awakening

A Euphoric Transmission

Son of Ma emerges with a sound that brings a new depth to the dancefloor, bridging uplifting trance with a message of spiritual awakening. Born from a profound journey through shame into the embrace of self-love, his music is a testament to the Cosmic Mother who guided him home. Weaving ethereal trance and hypnotic beats around sacred lyrics, he crafts powerful anthems for a global awakening. The result is a euphoric transmission for the dancefloor - a space for cathartic release and profound revelation, where every drop feels like a homecoming.
